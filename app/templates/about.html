About The Shirt Bar

Premium Menswear, Designed for the Modern Gentleman

The Shirt Bar is a Singapore-based menswear brand focused on timeless shirts, refined styling and everyday comfort for work, weekends and special occasions.

We believe a good shirt should feel effortless. Every piece is selected with attention to fit, fabric, versatility and long-term wearability.

From crisp office shirts to relaxed linen pieces, The Shirt Bar focuses on wardrobe essentials that are practical, polished and easy to wear in Singapore’s climate.

Refined Everyday Style

Shirts that look sharp without feeling overly formal, suitable for office wear, dinners, events and daily dressing.

Comfort for Singapore

Breathable fabrics, clean silhouettes and practical styling choices for warm, humid weather.

Customer First

A smoother online shopping experience helps customers browse, choose and check out with confidence.
